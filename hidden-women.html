The Queen who loved cats more than war.

A peak into the royal palace, where the empire ruled and cats reigned.

Queen Victoria ruled an empire,but her time was devoted to her pets, from dogs and cats to exotic birds to even a parrot..

Another example is Mary Anning, a fossil collector whose discoveries in paleontology helped shape modern science, but she was often ignored by male colleagues.

Why They Were Overlooked

Women faced strict societal expectations that limited education and access to professional fields. Their work was often published under male names or simply dismissed.

Why It Matters

Studying these women shows how history has gaps, and recognizing their contributions today gives a more accurate picture of the past. It also provides inspiration for future generations.
